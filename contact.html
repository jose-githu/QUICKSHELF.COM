Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Quickshelf

Delivery

How long does delivery take?

We offer multiple delivery options: Same-day delivery in Kimana Ward and Loitokitok area, next-day delivery across the region, and standard delivery within 2-3 business days. Delivery times are confirmed at checkout.

Delivery

Can I track my order in real-time?

Yes! All orders include real-time tracking. You'll receive a tracking number via SMS and can monitor your shipment 24/7 through our portal or mobile app.

Account

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click "Sign Up" on our homepage, enter your email and business details, set a password, and verify your email. You'll have instant access to our catalogue.

Account

Can I manage multiple accounts?

Yes! B2B customers can have multiple accounts for different departments or locations. Contact our sales team to set up additional accounts with separate billing and permissions.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfers, and cash on delivery for local orders in Kimana Ward and Loitokitok.

Payment

Do you offer payment plans?

Products
