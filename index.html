About Quickshelf

Quickshelf is revolutionizing supply chain management in Kenya by connecting businesses with reliable vendors through our innovative platform. Based in Kimana Town, Oloitokitok, we're committed to making procurement seamless, efficient, and accessible for businesses of all sizes.

Our Mission

We're building the supply chain superhighway for Kimana's entrepreneurs. From small retailers to hardware stores, we connect everyone to reliable suppliers, fast delivery, and smart inventory management.

Our Vision

To revolutionize commerce in rural Kenya by making every local shop in Kimana as efficient as a tech startup in Nairobi—because great business tools shouldn't be a city privilege.

Why Choose Us

With 99% on-time delivery, same-day service availability, and 24/7 support, we ensure your business never runs out of essential supplies. Our local presence means we understand your needs.

Local Presence

Proudly serving Kimana Town and surrounding areas within Oloitokitok Sub-County. We offer same-day delivery within a 15km radius and personalized support for every business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Quickshelf

How do I place an order?
Simply create a customer account, browse our catalogue, add items to your cart, and checkout. You can pay via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or cash on delivery. Orders placed before 2PM qualify for same-day delivery within Kimana.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa (most popular), bank transfers, and cash on delivery for local orders. For businesses, we offer flexible payment terms including 30-day credit lines for verified customers.
How can I become a vendor?
Click "Create Account," select "Vendor," and fill in your business details. You'll need a valid business license, M-Pesa account, and inventory list. We'll schedule a meeting to onboard you within 48 hours. No upfront fees—we only take a small commission on successful orders.
What areas do you deliver to?
We primarily serve Kimana Town and surrounding areas within Oloitokitok Sub-County. Our delivery network covers local shops, schools, and businesses within a 15km radius. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2PM.
How do I track my order?
Every order gets a unique tracking ID sent via SMS. You can track your order in real-time through your customer dashboard or by calling our support line. For local Kimana deliveries, we'll also send you the rider's contact for direct coordination.
