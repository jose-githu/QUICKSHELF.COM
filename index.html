Home FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I place an order?

Browse our catalogue, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can also contact us directly for bulk orders or custom requests. Our team is here to help you every step of the way.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, mobile money (M-Pesa), bank transfers, and PayPal. For business accounts, we also offer invoice-based payment terms.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 2-5 business days depending on your location. We offer same-day delivery for urgent orders within select areas. Track your order in real-time through our system.

Can I track my order?

Yes! You'll receive a tracking number once your order is shipped. You can monitor your delivery status in real-time through our website or by contacting our support team.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Products must be in original condition with packaging intact. Contact our support team to initiate a return and we'll guide you through the process.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Absolutely! We provide special pricing for bulk orders and business accounts. Contact our B2B team for custom quotes and volume discounts tailored to your needs.

How can I become a vendor?

We're always looking for quality vendors! Sign in with a vendor account or contact us at josephflynn774@gmail.com with details about your products and business. Our team will review your application and get back to you.
